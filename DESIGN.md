Create a high-end mobile app UI mockup for an app named "Printculture".

The app is a private cultural sharing platform designed for two people to exchange cultural discoveries (books, films, exhibitions, music) in an intimate and curated environment.

The design must feel premium, editorial, and artistic, inspired by modern cultural magazines, art galleries, and minimal Scandinavian interfaces.

---

🎨 DESIGN STYLE:

- Minimalist and refined
- Editorial / magazine-inspired layout
- Soft luxury aesthetic (not flashy, not startup-like)
- Intimate and calm atmosphere
- Strong typography hierarchy
- Generous whitespace

---

📱 SCREENS TO INCLUDE (4 mobile screens side by side in a 5.5 x 3 ratio composition):

1. Welcome screen (no photos, only typography and branding)
2. Home screen (curated cultural feed)
3. Product/Content detail screen (example: book, exhibition, or film)
4. Conversation screen (private exchange between two users)

Do not include anything else outside of the mockup (no text explanation, no annotations).

---

🎨 COLOR SYSTEM (strictly follow):

- Primary: deep bluish black (#1C1C28)
- Primary soft: (#2A2A3C)
- Accent: soft dusty rose (#D6AFA0)
- Accent light: (#E9D5CC)
- Neutral light background: (#F7F6F4)
- Neutral text hierarchy:
  - dark text (#1A1917)
  - medium text (#5B5853)
  - light text (#A8A39B)

Use soft contrast, avoid pure black and pure white.

---

🔤 TYPOGRAPHY:

- Titles: elegant serif font (Playfair Display style)
- Body: clean sans-serif (Inter style)
- Strong hierarchy:
  - Large editorial titles
  - Medium section headings
  - Small subtle metadata

Use letter spacing and line height to enhance readability and elegance.

---

📐 LAYOUT & SPACING:

- Generous padding (24px–32px)
- Rounded corners (16px–24px radius)
- Soft shadows only (very subtle)
- Vertical rhythm with consistent spacing
- Clean grid alignment

---

🧩 UI COMPONENTS:

Cards:

- Rounded (2xl)
- Light background or dark variant
- Include category label (EXHIBITION, FILM, BOOK)
- Title + short description
- Optional subtle image placeholder or abstract visual

Buttons:

- Rounded full pill shape
- Accent color background
- Minimal text

Navigation:

- Bottom navigation bar
- 5 items: Home, Search, Add (+), Messages, Profile
- Center button slightly elevated

Inputs:

- Rounded full
- Soft background (neutral)

---

🌙 DARK MODE ELEMENTS:
Include at least one dark-themed screen (detail screen or welcome screen):

- Dark background (#1C1C28)
- Light typography
- Accent used sparingly

---

🧠 UX DETAILS:

- The app is for TWO users only → make it feel personal
- Conversation screen should feel like a mix of chat + shared cultural cards
- Avoid social media patterns (no likes, no counters, no public metrics)
- Focus on sharing and discovery

---

✨ VISUAL MOOD:

- Calm, intellectual, curated
- Inspired by art books and gallery apps
- Slightly poetic, not overly functional

---

⚠️ IMPORTANT:

- No generic UI
- No bright colors
- No clutter
- No stock-heavy visuals
- No dashboard-style design

The final result must look like a premium cultural product ready for App Store featuring.
